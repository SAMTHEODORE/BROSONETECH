Privacy & Data

How we collect, use, store and protect your business & customer data

This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how long we keep it, and your rights. Last updated: August 10, 2025.

Contact
privacy@brosonetech.com
Governing law: India

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data:
    Name, business name, email, phone, billing address, GSTIN (if provided), and account credentials.
  • Transactional data:
    Invoices, payment records, customer details, product/service descriptions, taxes and GST details needed for invoicing and reporting.
  • Usage data & logs:
    IP addresses, device and browser details, activity logs, error reports to help improve service and security.
  • Support & communications:
    Messages, attachments and communications exchanged with support for troubleshooting or billing inquiries.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide services: Process invoices, generate GST reports, reconcile payments, and maintain your account.
  • Security & fraud prevention: Detect abuse, protect accounts, and respond to incidents.
  • Product improvements: Analyze usage to improve features and stability.
  • Legal & compliance: Retain and disclose information when required by law or for tax/GST obligations.

3. Sharing & Third Parties

  • Payment processors: We share necessary transaction details with payment gateways to process payments.
  • Service providers: Analytics, email delivery, backup and hosting providers operate under contract and only process data as instructed.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information to comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax authorities).
  • Aggregated data: Non-identifiable aggregated usage data may be used for analytics and product insights.

4. Security Measures

  • Encryption: Data at rest and in transit is protected using industry-standard encryption.
  • Access controls: Role-based access, least-privilege, and secure credentials for employees and service accounts.
  • Backups & redundancy: Regular backups and disaster recovery plans to protect your transactional records and GST reports.
  • Incident response: We maintain a process to investigate and notify affected users in case of a data incident as required by law.

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained for the duration of your subscription.
  • Post-cancellation: We retain data for 90 days to allow exports and reactivation unless legal retention (e.g., GST records) requires longer storage.
  • Deletion requests: You can request deletion; we will process requests subject to legal obligations and verification.

6. Your Rights

  • Access & portability: Request a copy of your personal data or export transactional data from the Data Export tool.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion subject to legal retention obligations (e.g., GST).
  • Objections & restrictions: You may object to certain processing or request restriction where applicable.
  • For requests, contact privacy@brosonetech.com. We may ask for verification to protect account security.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, analytics, and feature functionality. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser. Third-party services (analytics, payment) may set their own cookies.

8. GDPR, CCPA & International Users

If you are an EU/EEA or California resident, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA. We will honor these rights where they apply. Requests can be sent to privacy@brosonetech.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted with a new “Last updated” date. Continued use after updates indicates acceptance.
